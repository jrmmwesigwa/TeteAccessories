Our Story

Meet the team behind Tete Accessories

The Tete Accessories Journey

Founded in 2015 in Kampala, Tete Accessories began with a passion for creating unique, handcrafted jewelry that celebrates Ugandan craftsmanship and style.

Our founder, Sarah Nakimuli, started making jewelry as a hobby, inspired by the rich cultural heritage and vibrant colors of Uganda. What began as a small collection sold to friends and family has now grown into a beloved brand known for quality, creativity, and attention to detail.

The name "Tete" - meaning "shine" in the local dialect - reflects our mission to help every woman shine with confidence through our carefully crafted accessories.
